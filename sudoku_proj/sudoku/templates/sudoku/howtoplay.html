How to play Sudoku

Sudoku is a game that is both easy to learn and difficult to master. Puzzles can vary from extremely simple to seemingly impossible.
Each puzzle has a unique solution and you will never have to guess at answers. Rather than being a math game, Sudoku uses numbers
to create logic based puzzles which use the same set of consistent rules. This creates elegant and intuitive gameplay mechanics.

Your goal when playing Sudoku is to fill in the numbers 1-9 EXACTLY ONCE in each row, column, and 3x3 region.
